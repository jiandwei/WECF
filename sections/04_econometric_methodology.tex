\subsection{Specification}

The empirical specification is given by:
\begin{equation}
    Y_{it} = \alpha + \beta D_{it} + \gamma X_{it} + \delta_i + \theta_t + \varepsilon_{it}
    \label{eq:empirical_spec}
\end{equation}
where $\delta_i$ are individual fixed effects and $\theta_t$ are time fixed effects.
